Adolf Hitler

The man who destroyed million lives

Here's a time line of Adolf Hitler's life:

Early life:

  • 1889 - Born in Braunau am Inn, Austria
  • 1907 - Rejected from Vienna Academy of Fine Arts
  • 1914 - Enlists in the German Army during World War I
  • 1918 - Wounded in action and decorated for bravery

Rise to Power:

  • 1919 - Joins the Nazi Party and becomes known for his fiery speeches
  • 1921 - Becomes leader of the Nazi Party
  • 1923 - Leads the failed Beer Hall Putsch and is imprisoned for treason
  • 1924 - Writes Mein Kampf while in prison, outlining his racist and nationalist ideology
  • 1929 - The Great Depression fuels the popularity of the Nazi Party
  • 1932 - Becomes the most popular politician in Germany
  • 1933 - Appointed Chancellor of Germany, consolidating his power

Dictatorship and World War II:

  • 1934 - Eliminates political opponents in the Night of the Long Knives
  • 1935 - Abrogates the Treaty of Versailles and remilitarizes the Rhineland
  • 1938 - Annexes Austria and the Sudetenland
  • 1939 - Invades Poland, sparking World War II
  • 1940 - Invades France and occupies much of Europe
  • 1941 - Launches Operation Barbarossa, invading the Soviet Union
  • 1942-1945 - The tide of the war turns against Germany
  • 04/30/1945 - Commits suicide in Berlin as Allied forces close in
